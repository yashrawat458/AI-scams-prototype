Step 1. Data Harvesting

Goal: Gather raw material to make the scam believable.

Scammers collect:

  • Voice clips (social media videos, WhatsApp audios)
  • Photos/videos (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook)
  • Personal info (job, family, phone number)
  • Leaked data (emails, passwords, bank info)
  • Organizational details (who reports to whom)

AI's role: Helps scrape, organize, and profile victims fast. Even tiny data points become powerful when AI stitches them together.

Without data, there is no personalization. Without personalization, AI scams lose power.

Authority Fabrication

Scammer claims institutional power.

Examples: police, bank official, tax officer, courier, telecom, HR.

Distress Manipulation

Scammer performs vulnerability to trigger rescue instinct.

Examples: stranded traveler, medical emergency plea, charity fraud.

What did we learn?

Practical defences for each step of the scam.

Step 1
Data Harvesting
  • Limit what is publicly searchable.
  • Turn on multi-factor authentication.
  • Disable unnecessary app permissions.
  • Regularly monitor for breached credentials.
Step 2
Synthesis
  • Pre-agreed family passphrases for urgent requests.
  • Skepticism should be your default operating mode.
  • Verify identity of the caller through a second, independent, trusted channel.
Step 3
Emotional Triggers
  • Treat time as a warning signal if interaction stretches unusually long.
  • Refuse real-time compliance.
  • In moments of distress or urgency, seek external perspective.
  • Reject sunk-cost thinking.
  • Separate emotional intimacy from financial/informational access.
  • Check for isolation language: "don't tell anyone," "act now," "only you."
Step 4
Extraction
  • Search exact phrasing online — scammer scripts repeat.
  • Impose a mandatory delay before any transfer or doc sharing.
  • Never share passwords under any circumstance.
  • Use only official institutional channels; never links or alternate payment methods.
  • Trust built earlier is irrelevant at the extraction moment — reassess from zero.
