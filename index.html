Data Harvesting

Goal: Gather raw material to make the scam believable.


Scammers collect:

  • Voice clips (social media videos, WhatsApp audios)
  • Photos/videos (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook)
  • Personal info (job, family, phone number)
  • Leaked data (emails, passwords, bank info)
  • Organizational details (who reports to whom)

AI's role: Helps scrape, organize, and profile victims fast. Even tiny data points become powerful when AI stitches them together.


Without data, there is no personalization. Without personalization, AI scams lose power.
